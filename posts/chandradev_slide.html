The Story of Chandra Dev

A Mythological Tale

Respect for same-sex relationships is evident in various stories from the Vedas and Puranas. One such story is that of Chandra Dev, which highlights different aspects of his life.

тЬЕ 1. The Conflict Between Chandra Dev and Tara
  • Chandra Dev abducted Tara, who was the wife of Brihaspati.
  • This led to a great conflict among the gods and sages.
  • A war almost broke out between Brihaspati and Chandra Dev, but it was stopped by the intervention of the gods.
  • Tara was returned to Brihaspati, but she was already pregnant.
тЬЕ 2. The Birth of Budh
  • Tara gave birth to a radiant son.
  • Brihaspati asked who the father was. Initially, Tara remained silent, but later revealed that it was Chandra's son.
  • The child was named Budh, who became renowned for wisdom and intellect.
тЬЕ 3. Ila's Curse and Gender Transformation
  • King Sudhyumna once entered the forest of Shiva and Parvati.
  • Due to a curse, he was transformed into a woman (Ila).
  • Later, Shiva granted a boon that Ila would alternate between being a woman for one month and a man for the next.
тЬЕ 4. The Marriage of Budh and Ila
  • While Ila was in her female form, Budh fell in love with her.
  • They got married and had a son named Pururava, who became the founder of the Lunar Dynasty (Chandravansh).
  • Budh accepted Ila's alternating forms with love and understanding.
